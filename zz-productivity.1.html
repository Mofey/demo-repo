Being lazy and still productive

4 ways to be lazy and still be productive as an employee

As I have established previously, being 'lazy' in this concept doesn't mean the absence of work, it rather identifies with achieving goals without expending much energy and having loads of free time. As employees, we have to meet lots of targets and keep up our best behavior at work in order to get rewards such as pay raises, bonuses and others. We want to gain such rewards but don't believe in 'stressing' out, I believe these tips will come in handy.

1. Firstly, steer clear of tardiness. Punctuality is a well-known success factor of any employee. Don't get me wrong, I know it takes a lot of 'effort' in cultivating a habit of punctuality but remember I mentioned that we still need to put in work to be 'lazy' successfully. Punctuality might look demanding but consider its gains. It allows for adequate preparation before work and relieves unnecessary pressure during work hours. In the long run, punctuality saves energy.

2. Secondly, a standard schedule is priceless. As mentioned in the previous post, having a well-arranged plan of activities for the day helps identify task to be done earlier on. Once these tasks are completed, freedom at last.

3. My third recommendation is being updated at all times. This allows us to be informed of sudden changes at work and lets us adjust accordingly. Having a system for getting information as they come is essential. Another tip is perfecting your skill. Being better at our tasks at work save a lot of time. This also allows us to enjoy the job more. Improving helps a lot in the long run and it may lead to other rewards like promotions etc. Maintaining good office relations is also very important to work without stress. Being friendly allows a smooth and peaceful atmosphere to strive in the workplace. It also helps the whole organization. Having another source of earnings helps in increasing productivity. More income means more investment in one's self and this improves the quality of his work. Other sources of income allow to maintain good labor quality at work.

4. Lastly, self-discipline is highly recommended. Self-discipline allows one to picture generally gain ahead of temporary short-term gains. This ensures we put in the required amount of work and naturally, gain due rewards. Wanting to be 'lazy' still requires work that will show its desired effect in the long run. That's why self-discipline is required to fully utilize the above tips to gain desired results. Thanks for reading, remember to share
