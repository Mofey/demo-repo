Benefits of abstaining from premarital sex

5 Reasons why you should abstain from premarital sex

Less value is placed on virginity today compared to some Centuries ago. Some say virginity is a mere lack of opportunity and that it is not purity in any sense. Sex between singles is called fornication, while sex between single and married or marrieds is called adultery and both are sin against God. There are many reasons why you should abstain from premarital sex, below are 5 major reasons that I think you should know:

1. Risk of pregnancy: there is nothing like the fear of unwanted pregnancy. Your mind is always at rest even if there is a delay in your monthly period (that’s for ladies only). It is not the number of times you engage in having sex that determines whether you will be pregnant or not. Your first attempt may result into pregnancy. Oh, you say “but there is condom out there.” Well, let me say even the manufacturers of condom do not guarantee 100% protection, why then do you want to take the risk when you are not ready to be a nursing mother or father.

2. Possibility of contracting STDs: there are possibilities of contracting STDs. STDs are not written on faces. So, you cannot easily guess who has it. When you abstain from premarital sex you get rid of fear of contracting STDs.

3. High risk of disappointment: many singles that have allowed others the taste of their juice have high risk of facing disappointment from the other party because there’s nothing new to see anymore if they get married to each other afterwards. That’s what the other guys always feel after having sex with a lady and due to this, they would rather walk away because there is nothing really to value in such body any longer.

4. Self-respect: you have a great sense of value within yourself. No one will look down on you. In fact, you earn respect from people around you. You feel confident when you are sharing your past with your partner.

5. Premarital sex is a sin against God: your body is God’s temple. When you have sexual intercourse outside marriage, you defile God’s temple. Sin invokes God’s anger and sexual sin between singles is a sin against God and the bodies of the individuals that engaged in such act.

In Conclusion
When you value your body and honor God with your body and He will honor you. Sex is sweeter and better enjoyed when you are married. Why do it now with all above consequences? Does the consequences worth the temporary pleasure? May His Grace be with you.
