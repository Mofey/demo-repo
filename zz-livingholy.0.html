Overcoming the temptations of sin in college

10 EASY WAYS TO OVERCOME THE TEMPTATIONS OF SIN IN COLLEGE

If there is one thing God really abhors that’s SIN. But sin is a weapon in the devil’s hand used to handicap man (most especially youngsters). Temptation comes first before sin itself. (It could be fueled by the activities in the mind or by external factors). Whichever it is that generates the temptation, the focal point is there is always a breeding ground for every temptation to sin. Temptation to sin is an ever-present danger to college student.

What are the sources of temptation?
These are succinctly summarized into 3 different categories as stated in 1 JOHN 2:16

  • LUST OF THE EYES
  • LUST OF THE FLESH
  • VAIN PRIDE OF LIFE

Every sin can be grouped into one or two of these categories.

Here in this post we will discuss 10 different ways to overcoming temptation of sin. Practical example is Eve in the garden.

Here are possible practical ways to overcome temptation to sin

1. IDENTIFY THE SOURCE(S) OF THE TEMPTATION AND CALL IT BY ITS NAME (No Rationalization)
2. BUILDING PERSONAL AND SCRIPTURAL CONVICTIONS ON CERTAIN CONTROVERSIAL ISSUES – christian dressing, sex outside marriage, alcohol consumption, abortion, music etc
3. AVOID EVERY APPEARANCE THAT TRIGGERS SUCH TEMPTATION – 2Timothy 2:22
4. SURROUND YOURSELF WITH PEOPLE OF LIKE MINDS THAT WANT TO STAND FOR GOD – 1 CORINTHIANS 15:33
5. OCCUPY YOUR MIND WITH GOD’S WORD – Psalm 119:11
6. DO NOT GIVE SECOND THOUGHT TO THAT THOUGHT – Proverbs 4:23
7. DEAL WITH LUST WITHIN YOU – James 1:14
8. BE WATCHFUL AND PRAYERFUL – 1 peter 5:7; Revelation 12:12
9. MAKE YOUR SELF ACCOUNTABLE TO SOMEONE HIGHER THAN YOU IN SPIRITUAL ISSUES OR YOUR PARENTS
10. REMEMBER ALWAYS THAT “ YOU CANNOT BE TESTED BEYOND YOUR CAPACITY” – 1 Corinthians 10:13
