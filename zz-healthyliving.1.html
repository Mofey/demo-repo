Benefis of taking lots of fruits

8 benefits of taking fruits every day

Fruit is an important part of a healthy diet. It is an important source of dietary fiber, folic acid, vitamins, potassium and antioxidants which are needed for every individual in order to have healthy lives.

1. Weight management: Fruits are rich in dietary fiber which can help regulate weight loss. Fruits also contain less calories (for example, watermelon, lemons, bananas and apples). When you take fruits instead of food, you consume less calories which is great for those who wants to reduce or maintain their weight.

2. It helps your blood pressure/heart: Fruits rich in potassium are necessary for the normal functioning of all cells in the human body. It regulates the heart beat and ensures proper function of the muscles and nerves. High potassium consumption from fruits may lower blood pressure and reduce the risk of heart diseases and strokes. Potassium could help remove extra sodium from your body through your urine which is a good thing, because too much of sodium could cause high blood pressure. Bananas, avocado are fruits that are very rich in potassium.

3. Pregnant women: Some fruits contain folic acid which are very beneficial for women when taken before, during and after pregnancy. Folic acid is a manmade form of B vitamin called folate. Folate plays an important role in the production of red blood cells and helps your baby's neural tube develop into their brain and spinal cord. Folate is found naturally in dark green vegetables and citrus fruits. When taken before and during pregnancy, fruits which contain folic acid may protect pregnant women against miscarriage, premature birth and other pregnancy complications.

4. It keeps you well hydrated: Every cell, tissue and organ in your body requires water to function properly. Though drinking water is most often the best and cheapest way to stay hydrated, water can also be found in fruits and vegetables (for example watermelons, tomatoes etc.) which can keep us hydrated. Being well hydrated is crucial for many reasons; to regulate body temperature, prevent infections, deliver nutrients to cells, improve sleep quality and mood.

5. It can help you prevent or fight cancer: Fruits that are rich in antioxidants can help to prevent or fight cancer. Antioxidants are substances that may protect your cells against free radicals, which may play a role in cancer and other diseases. Free radicals are produced when your body breaks down food or when you’re exposed to tobacco smoke or radiation. Such exposure can result to cell damage. To lower your risk for many types of cancer – aim to build your diet around a variety of antioxidant rich fruit and vegetable.

6. Regulates bowel movement: Fruits rich in fiber can help prevent or relieve constipation, helping waste to move smoothly through the body. It regulates bowel movements and reduces the time that waste spends inside the intestines.

7. Fruits can make us strong and energetic: Fruits are anytime snacks, as they are easy to carry and are loaded with vitamins, minerals and fiber and can give you an immediate energy boost. If you’re tired, worn out and stressed, consuming fruits will enable you regain that energy.

8.Body immunity: Feeding your body fruits may help keep your immune system strong.
